    Connect with your family, friends

    out when something important

    Always know why the cruiser
    is on the road.

    If there's commotion like police
    activity, land cruisers on the road, or
    road closures, pull up the website and
    know why instantly.

    Know the real story
    faster.

    Fast, accurate information makes a
    meaningful difference in emergencies.

    KitereSafety alerts go out instantly. This speed is
    critical in acting on important events like
    abductions and missing children or elderly people.

    When you can, contribute to help resolve a
    situation.

    You used to have to go to the police station or call a
    police tip line to help. Now you can use KitereSafety
    to share relevant updates with others. You can also
    leave comments, which will help locate missing
    people and share information about natural disasters.

    For the
    greater good.

    Hospital and emergency
    responders save lives with
    faster updates.

    Fire departments can use KitereSafety to
    direct resources more effectively
    because they can see the intensity
    of a fire on a photo, while
    doctors and nurses can be prepared to
    receive patients before even getting a call.

    Increasing transparency
    between the community and its
    residents.

    We believe we should unite against
    crime. Everyone deserves to be
    safe--and now, everyone can better look
    out for each other. Having real-time
    information available and open to
    everyone empowers us all to make
    smarter decisions to stay safe.

    Everyone is safer when everyone
    has the same access.

    KitereSafety is for everyone--and is
    therefore completely independent. With
    KitereSafety, everyone in the community,
    whether a resident or member of law
    enforcement, has the same access to the
    website and its unbiased information.
